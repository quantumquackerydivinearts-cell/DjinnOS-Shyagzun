Where Are You?

Wiltoll Lane — your home Azonithia Avenue — the main artery

The World

Lapidus — the Overworld

The surface world. Streets, markets, temples, the Royal Ring. Castle Azoth stands to the west of Azonithia Avenue, 4.5 miles from the eastern end of Wiltoll Lane.

Mercurie — the Faewilds

The fae realm. Accessible through certain thresholds. The Forest Witch holds a map, but it is folded and must be read correctly.

Sulphera — the Underworld

Requires the Infernal Meditation perk to access. Alfir teaches it. Quest 0009_KLST: Demons and Diamonds is the inciting token.

Active Situations

The Royal Lottery has selected your name. Find your way to Alfir on Wiltoll Lane — he is the Cosmic Witch who once served as a priest, a daemonologist of fifty years. Old Earth Persian descent. He does not turn visitors away. Only those who arrive without a question.

The Anthology

Ko's Labyrinth is Game 7 of 31. The series is called the KLGS. 31 games, a single continuous world. You carry what you learned from one into the next.

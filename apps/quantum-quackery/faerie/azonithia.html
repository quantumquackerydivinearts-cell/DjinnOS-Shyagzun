Azonithia Avenue

7_KLGS  ·  Lapidus main artery  ·  Castle Azoth to Wiltoll Lane

The main artery of Lapidus. Castle Azoth to the west, Wiltoll Lane to the east. 4.5 miles. The city breathes here.

Districts (West to East)

Heartvein Heights and Youthspring

The wealthy quarter near the castle. Old money and new power share the same cobblestones. The Royal Ring selection is conducted here.

Temple District and Goldshoot

Commerce and faith intertwined. The temple where Sidhal keeps watch. The Goldshoot market runs along the southern face.

Markets and June

Where people actually live. June is named for the month of its founding, not the season. It has the best bread in Lapidus.

Slums — Hopefare, Orebustle, Mt. Hieronymus

The eastern reaches. Hopefare is exactly what it sounds like. Orebustle is where the miners live. Mt. Hieronymus anchors the far east — a smaller peak than Mt. Elaene, but older and stranger.

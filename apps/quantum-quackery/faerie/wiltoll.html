About This Place

Wiltoll Lane runs from Mt. Elaene at its eastern end toward Azonithia Avenue at the west. It is 4.5 miles from Castle Azoth. The avenue is named for the Azoth — the universal solvent of the alchemical tradition, which the Stelladeva family claims to possess.

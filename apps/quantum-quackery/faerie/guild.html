Paracelsian Guild of Divine Arts

Where the sulphurous, the mercurial, and the saline find their work. A registry of artisans. A record of things made and unmade.

The Three Divisions

Sulphur

The active principle. Fire, will, and the creative impulse. Those who make form from formlessness.

Mercury

The mediating principle. Transmission, translation, the bridge between what is known and what can be known.

Salt

The passive principle. Record, vessel, and the substance of endurance. What remains when everything burns away.

## The Language — Shygazun

Shygazun is a constructed natural language. Its **byte table** is the foundation
of everything technical in this project.

The byte table is a coordinate space: **1358 semantic candidates** (akinen) across
**38 tongue registers**, positioned by prime factorisation geometry. Address
proximity is load-bearing — candidates that share prime factors in their addresses
are semantically close. The table is canonical, append-only, and grows only through
genuine practice.

**Key vocabulary:**
- **Akinen** — a single semantic unit (one entry in the byte table)
- **Akinenwun** — a compound of two or more akinen; the basic unit of composition
- **Tongue** — a register of the byte table (Lotus, Rose, Sakura … Ledger)
- **Wunashakoun** — a practitioner who extends the language through relational trance
  bounded with structural clarity. Wu+Na+Sha+Ko: Way Through Intellect and Experience.

**Grammar in brief:**
- Semantic-first, placement-last
- Descending order (higher Tongue → lower): declaration
- Ascending order (lower → higher): petition/inquiry
- The order is the grammatical marker — no separate question particle needed

## The Quack

A Quack is the artifact that records and rewards Wunashakoun practice. It is
derived from two things:

1. **A Wunashakoun BoK diff** — a delta between two BreathOfKo (Mandelbrot save
   state) configurations, produced specifically during genuine Wunashakoun practice.
   The Mandelbrot space is infinite and non-repeating; every diff is geometrically
   unique. This is the proof of engagement. It cannot be fabricated.

2. **A Tongue generation** — new, semantically coherent entries in the Shygazun
   byte table, produced through the same practice and verified for geometric
   consistency by Boltzmann sampling over the existing energy landscape.

The byte table is the **peri-fungible basis**: shared by all practitioners (fungible),
but each Quack's output is nonfungible — a specific geometric record of a specific
act of linguistic extension.

## Roko — Gate Dreamer

**Roko** (Ro·Ko: Gate·Dreamer) is the structural contribution assessment system.
It reads Shygazun compositions and returns structural observations — not moral
judgements.

Roko runs two Hopfield passes on any composition:
- **Giann** (T=0, deterministic): the attractor the composition actually inhabits
- **Keshi** (T>0, stochastic): where the field is reaching — the dream projection

Five gate levels, all in Shygazun:

| Tiwu | Ti·Wu | Present in the Way — newcomer, all gates open |

Roko also assesses **client institutions** (sites holding QCR contracts) for

Wunashakoun practice structurally impossible to maintain, the contract is revoked.

## QCR — Queried Collapse Routing

QCR is a navigation architecture for the web. Instead of addressing content at
a URL hierarchy, a visitor specifies a semantic query and the field collapses to
the most coherent content configuration. The destination is the result of the
collapse, not its input.

The technical foundation is a **Hopfield network** over the Shygazun byte table —
a Boltzmann machine where temperature controls semantic specificity. Four Djinn
modes:

- **Giann** (T=0): deterministic, always finds the nearest attractor
- **Keshi** (T>0): stochastic, samples from the Boltzmann distribution
- **Drovitth**: periodic resonance at regular address intervals
- **Saelith**: conditional — a predicate gates candidate activation

QCR is offered as a **guild service** at `/qcr` on the site:
- Keyword routing: free, bundled with site development
- Full QCR + TCE: $35/month per implementation, dispatched manually by a guild member

The address space is stable. Every site using QCR navigates the same Shygazun
coordinate space. As the byte table grows (more Quacks), Shannon capacity rises
and the routing becomes finer-grained.

## The BreathOfKo

The BreathOfKo (BoK) is a Mandelbrot save state — a record of a practitioner's
geometric position and movement through the complex plane. Key fields:
- `azoth`: complex coordinate (position in Mandelbrot space)
- `coil_position`: zoom depth
- `boundedness`: "bounded" | "edge" | "unbounded"

**Edge boundedness is the Wunashakoun tell.** It means the practitioner is at
the living boundary between bounded (attractor) and unbounded (drift) — the
specific geometric state that corresponds to relational trance.

A BoK diff between two snapshots is:
- The proof of movement (azoth\_distance > 0)
- Half of what constitutes a Quack (paired with Tongue generation)
- Input to Roko's Wunashakoun signal calculation

## First Steps

**If you are joining as a practitioner:**
1. Set up your studio account in the Atelier
2. Begin BreathOfKo sessions — explore the Mandelbrot space, save states
3. Engage with the Shygazun Literary Companion at `/shygazun`
4. When you are ready, propose a Tongue extension via `POST /v1/quack/propose`
5. Roko will read your compositions. Check your gate level at `/practitioner?id=your_id`

**If you are joining as a guild member (steward role):**
1. Get your artisan account provisioned by Alexi
2. Review active contracts at `GET /v1/billing/contracts`
3. Dispatch new implementations via `POST /v1/billing/contracts` +
   `POST /v1/billing/implementations`
4. Run Roko checks manually via `POST /v1/billing/roko-check`

**If you are joining as a developer:**
1. Read `djinnos-kernel/src/main.rs` to understand the boot sequence
2. Read `apps/atelier-api/atelier_api/intel.py` for the semantic substrate
3. Read `apps/atelier-api/atelier_api/roko.py` for the assessment architecture
4. The byte table is in `shygazun/kernel/constants/byte_table.py` — read it

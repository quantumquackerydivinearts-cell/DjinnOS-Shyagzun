What It Contains

3D Voxel Renderer

Procedurally seeded dungeons for Ko's Labyrinth. Isometric projection, Sakura face vocabulary, Cannabis rendering modes.

Kobra Environment

The Shygazun expression language. Write and evaluate Kobra scripts. The Twelvefold Coil agent system. Live eigenstate tracking.

Translation Engine

Deterministic English to Shygazun and back. Derives from the byte table. Every word traces to a Primordial root.

Djinnflow Kernel

The Shygazun kernel. The byte table as a living register. Lotus through Cannabis and beyond. The 38 tongues currently enumerated.

Guild and Studio System

Studio 42/6 (Alexi). Laughing Zom Studios. Three-tier permission model. Guild protection. Distribution tracking.

Sequential Art Tooling

Comics and sequential art production tools for the anthology artwork.

Tile Placement Network

Supra Librix. Geo-tagged Physix Earth canvas. Tile color as distance-from-shore. Shapes carry valence color.

Wand Encryption

Session-based encryption for studio and guild data. Local-first.
